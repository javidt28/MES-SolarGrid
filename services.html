Our Services

Innovative solutions for a sustainable future

Renewable Energy Solutions

Harness sustainable power with solar and renewable options tailored to your energy needs.

We design and install hybrid solar systems using Victron Energy inverters, batteries, and solar equipment. Systems are sized to your consumption and goals, with optional battery storage for backup and self-consumption. Each installation is connected to Victron VRM so you and our team can monitor performance in real time—all visible on our Solar Performance Portal.

Air Conditioning Solutions

We specialise in designing, installing, and maintaining systems for reliable cooling, energy savings, and a comfortable indoor environment. We offer eco-friendly R290 and R32 options for sustainable cooling with reliable performance.

Electrical Services

From installations to repairs and upgrades, our team delivers electrical solutions for residential, commercial, and industrial clients. We ensure safety and efficiency in every project and are your trusted partner for comprehensive electrical services.

Energy Audits & Consulting

Optimise your energy use with our audits and consulting. We help you reduce costs and improve sustainability by streamlining how you consume energy.
