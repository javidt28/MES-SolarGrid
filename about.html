About Us

Lighting the way to a greener future

Who We Are

Modern Electrical Solutions is Grenada’s leading solar company, dedicated to revolutionising the way we generate and consume energy.

We are a Grenadian company at the forefront of the renewable energy and sustainable cooling revolution, committed to a greener, more energy-efficient future. We specialise in hybrid solar systems using Victron Energy equipment and offer real-time visibility into installations through the Victron VRM portal. This Solar Performance Portal gives our team and clients a single place to access live dashboards for every system we monitor.

By the Numbers

  • 2011 — Year established
  • 20+ — Years of experience
  • 100+ — Projects completed
  • 1.5 MW — PV installations capacity

Our Vision

We take pride in our commitment to environmental sustainability. Our focus extends beyond installations—we offer energy audits and consulting to optimise energy use, reduce costs, and contribute to a greener Grenada. We aim to build trusted partnerships through exceptional service, becoming the go-to choice for reliable, efficient, and environmentally conscious electrical solutions.
